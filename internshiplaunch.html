SAVE THE DATE | Youth of Enterprise (YOE) Internship Programme Launch

We are pleased to invite you to the formal launch of our Youth of Enterprise (YOE) Internship Programme. This virtual event is scheduled to hold on Thursday, 12 May 2021, at 1 PM (West African Time).


It is without doubt that Nigeria has experienced a sustained period of economic growth in the last decade but this is without a corresponding improvement in employment. Research work conducted in the second quarter of 2020 showed that the unemployment rate in Nigeria stands at 27.1%, and at that time it was estimated to reach 32.5% at the end of 2021. This figure was projected to further increase in 2022 particularly with the youth unemployment rate taking the chunk of that with 50% and this comes to about 25-30m Nigerian youth.



The Youth of Enterprise (YOE) Internship Programme is a flagship initiative of EnterpriseNGR designed to help address national youth unemployment and enhance Nigeria’s future productive capacity. YOE aims to improve the skillsets, professional competence, and employability of young graduates and place them in high-value internships, thus positioning them for meaningful career journeys.


The YOE Internship Programme is a paid six-month programme that offers Nigerian youth the perfect opportunity to re-invent themselves and “GREENSTART” their careers.
