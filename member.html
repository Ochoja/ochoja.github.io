Be a
Member
EnterpriseNGR is the only industry-led organization representing the entire Financial and Professional Services sector.
We offer our members the opportunity to expand their business priorities and partake in important policy debates that shape economic growth. They also get to engage with policymakers and key industry stakeholders domestically and internationally.
Our members come from various sub-sectors of the Financial & related Professional services sector which include Banking, Asset Management, Capital Markets & Green Finance, Insurance, FinTech, Professional Services (Law, Accountancy, Consulting), Islamic Finance, and Business Process Outsourcing
MEMBERSHIP BENEFITS
We currently have 2 membership categories:
Founding Members
₦100m One-off
These are custodians of the EnterpriseNGR Vision and as such will be given voting rights. They will be granted lifetime membership as their future membership fees will be waived in view of their seed fund contributions.
Ordinary Members
GOLD (₦10m annually)
SILVER (₦5m annually)
BRONZE (₦1m annually)
Institutions in the Nigerian financial and related professional services sector who pay the annual membership fee. They shall have rights to vote additional Board members.
Membership is open to all players in the FPS Sector.
MEMBERSHIP BENEFITS
What you get as a member of EnterpriseNGR:
Leadership and Governance
Members have several opportunities to participate in the governance leadership of the organisation. This includes via involvement in various member committees on thematic issues in Financial and Professional Services sector as well as other member focused events and activities.
Policy Consultation and Development
Members have the opportunity to influence policy by engaging government, policy makers and senior stakeholders on key issues facing the Nigeria-based financial and professional services industry and their customers.
Events and Stakeholder Engagement
EnterpriseNGR hosts events to engage with key stakeholders and position the sector in line with its objective to be the Financial Centre for Africa. Members have the opportunity to host and attend EnterpriseNGR events in varying capacities.
Recognition and Branding
Members and member organizations are given recognition during events and stakeholder engagements; members’ logo and / profile are included in the member recognition page on the website and communication platforms and materials.
Consideration for Members’ Interests
EnterpriseNGR will pursue interests and aspirations of members. An annual membership survey will collate feedback on what members consider most important within the broader set of EnterpriseNGR’s focused areas.
Financial Management and Accountability
By engaging best governance practices, the management of EnterpriseNGR will exercise prudential management of resources and make available to members its annual financial statements.
Individual Member Engagement
Continuous engagements with members through a designated contact, usually the directorate of corporate affairs. This avenue will provide notifications and updates on EnterpriseNGR work and keep them abreast on new developments on a regular basis.
Opportunities for Businesses
Member organizations with relevant competencies will be engaged to implement EnterpriseNGR’s major projects, including national and regional projects commissioned in partnership with global donor agencies
