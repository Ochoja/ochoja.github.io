EnterpriseNGR is a member-led advocacy group that promotes the growth and development of Nigeria’s Financial and Professional Services (FPS) sector.

An economy with exponential potential.
Nigeria is the Largest Economy in Africa.
The Financial and Professional Services (FPS) sector has the potential to be the largest tax payer, largest employer, biggest exporting industry and largest contributor to total economic output. The sector, however, needs to work in unison and speak with one voice to achieve these goals.
Nigeria has the potential to be Africa’s premier financial center. To realize this potential, however, the Nigerian FPS sector needs to work collaboratively in a deliberate, concerted effort to foster an enabling and stable policy environment that promotes an open market and competitiveness.
WHAT WE DO
Promoting the Nigerian Financial and Professional Services Sector.
EnterpriseNGR is positioned to engage in both private sector and public sector advocacy with the aim of fostering a favourable operating environment for the Financial and Professional Services sector and championing the transformation of Nigeria into an international financial center.
