Our
Agenda
To position Nigeria as a leading financial centre in Africa and the world.
We aim to advance this agenda by actively collaborating with our members, industry partners, and stakeholders.
We focus our activities on key areas that affect our members and where our cross-sectoral remit allows us to make the greatest impact. These are:
  • Financial Inclusion.
  • Digitization.
  • Regionalization.
  • International trade and investment.
  • Sustainability.
We aim to advance this agenda by actively collaborating with our members, industry partners, and stakeholders. Our pursuit of recognizable success in all these areas will be supported by strong and reliable research and data insight – all aimed at supporting the growth of the Nigerian FPS sector and positioning it as a leading financial centre in Africa and the world.
A breakdown of our key focus areas
These are the tools we will deploy to succeed at our key focus areas and achieve the mission and vision of EnterpriseNGR.
1.
Financial Inclusion
Ensure that individuals and businesses have access to appropriate, affordable, and timely financial products and services, some of which include banking, loan, equity insurance products, etc.
2.
Digitization
Birth ideas to improve the adoption of technology across various sub-sectors of the Nigerian Financial and Professional Services (FPS) sector.
3.
Regionalization
Boost the expansion of Nigerian FPS operators across the continent and position Nigeria to tap the benefits of the African Continental Free Trade Area.
4.
International trade and investment
Publicize Nigeria as a major destination for international trade and investment and enable easy transactions.
5.
Sustainability
Encourage the sector to marry environmental, social, and governance (ESG) principles into their operations and investment philosophies.
Membership
Join our network of members at the forefront of advancing the development and transformation of Nigeria.
Our Action Plan
Putting Words to Action
These are the tools we will deploy to succeed at our key focus areas and achieve the mission and vision of EnterpriseNGR.
1.
Research & Policy
Establish thought leadership through robust research and publications, speaking at key industry events, and strategic media appearances across key online and offline platforms.
2.
Advocacy
We will ensure regular stakeholder engagement, organize regular events, and participate in key sector events to further solidify EnterpriseNGR’s position.
3.
Partnership
We will build strong partnerships with other associations within the sector and international organizations to accelerate the achievement of our objectives. Currently, we are establishing partnerships across research, advocacy, and the Youth of Enterprise Internship Program (YOE).
4.
Intervention
EnterpriseNGR will intervene in key sectors of the economy through driving joint investment allocation, promoting investment, and proprietary initiatives amongst others. To be the change agent for the growth we are advocating for.
5.
Standards
We will advocate for the highest standards in EnterpriseNGR and the sector while delivering quality self-regulatory initiatives hinged on evidence and global standards.
6.
Talent
The success of the Nigerian FPS sector depends on the quality of talent within, hence putting mechanisms in place to grow this talent is crucial. That is why we created the ‘Youth Of Enterprise’ Internship Programme (YOE) – an initiative that aims to build a strong pipeline of entry-level talent through the recruitment and upskilling of 5,000 interns annually and subsequently grow that number to 100,000 in the next 5 years.
