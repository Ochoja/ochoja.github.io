About
Us
An economy with exponential potential.
We are an independent member-led group established for the purpose of promoting and influencing an enabling policy environment for the Nigerian Financial and Professional Services (FPS) sector.
The Nigerian FPS sector comprises the Banking and Finance, Insurance, Pensions, Asset Management, Capital Markets and Green Finance, Islamic Finance, FinTech, Law, Accounting and Consulting subsectors.

Our goal is to support the growth and development of the Nigerian financial and professional services sector, the economy, and our members.

Nigeria’s Financial and Professional S ervices (FPS) sector contributes majorly to the nation’s economic growth and hence is crucial to enabling the positive development of the economy. Yet, amongst the many subsectors within Nigeria’s FPS, only the banking subsector has made giant strides in the last few decades achieving about 60% penetration in Nigeria with strong African footprints and international presence in key financial markets.


This growth needs to be sustained and replicated across other subsectors if we hope to advance the impact of the FPS, greatly strengthen the economy, and position the sector as a prime financial center for Africa. To do this, the sector needs an enabling and stable policy environment built, grown, and maintained by deliberate effort.


This is where EnterpriseNGR comes in.

Nurturing an enabling environment for engagement.
EnterpriseNGR nurtures an enabling environment for engagement with policymakers and champions business sector advocacy domestically and internationally. This is while working together with government authorities and key stakeholders to achieve positive outcomes that would lead to economic growth and development.
Supporting the formation of policies
As a consolidated advocacy group, we support government and regulators in the formation of policies that improve the financial inclusion and performance of FPS sector operators in Nigeria. These policies will result in increased tax receipts for the government, increased credit to the real sector, and overall increased employment opportunities. We take pride in doing this while protecting the interest of our members.

How We Started

As a consolidated advocacy group, we support government and regulators in the formation of policies that improve the financial inclusion and performance of FPS sector operators in Nigeria. These policies will result in increased tax receipts for the government, increased credit to the real sector, and overall increased employment opportunities. EnterpriseNGR was founded by some of the sector's key players; its members comprise industry professionals from various subsectors of the Nigerian FPS sector.


Our focus is to transform Nigeria into the premier financial services center in Africa, improve financial literacy and inclusion, and significantly impact the quality of life of Nigerians. We leverage the expertise of our members to tap the nation’s teeming economic potential in a bid to develop a strong and resilient economy.
